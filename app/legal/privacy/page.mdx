# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 2025-09-01**

This Policy explains how SavedIt handles information.

**Data We Process.** Account information, saved links and associated metadata, diagnostics, and basic analytics. We do not sell your data.

**How We Use Data.** To operate and improve the Service, provide support, and protect users.

**Analytics.** We use privacy‑respecting analytics to understand usage. You can opt out via your browser’s Do Not Track settings where supported.

**Security.** We use industry‑standard safeguards. No method of transmission is 100% secure.

**Your Choices.** You can request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at hello@savedit.app.
